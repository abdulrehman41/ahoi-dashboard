Getting the most out of your bot

To get the most out of your bot, you should provide as much information as possible. This will help the bot provide more accurate information to the user. The more information you provide, the better the bot will be able to assist the user.

Treat the bot like a new employee. It has a lot of general information and can make inferences based on that information. However, it doesn't know anything about your business or what you do, specifically. All information provided here will be given to the bot to help it make better decisions. Just like a new employee, if you don't provide the information, the bot won't be able to help the user as well as it could.

There is no such thing as too much information. Everything you add, no matter how small, will help. That said, quality information is better than quantity. If you provide a lot of information, but it's not accurate, the bot will repeat those mistakes.

Everything provided WILL be accessible to anyone calling in. Do not provide any sensitive information. The bot is not secure and should not be used to store any sensitive data.

Useful if you have a store or office location that the user may want to visit. Completely optional. Note that if you provide it here, it will be accessible to anyone calling in.

This is a simple way to provide question and answer training to the bot. Treat it like an FAQ or a knowledge base. The bot will use this information to help answer user questions. Consider any question that a receptionist or customer service representative might be asked.

Transfers allows the bot send the user to another phone number. This is useful if the user needs to speak to a human or if the bot can't answer the user's questions. Be very descriptive with names, as this will help the bot know when and who it can transfer to.

Any phone number provided here will be accessible to anyone calling in. The bot will not get the number directly, only the name, but anyone will be able to initiate the transfer.

These personality traits will be passed to the bot to help it provide a more human-like experience to the user. The bot will use these traits to determine how to respond to the user.
